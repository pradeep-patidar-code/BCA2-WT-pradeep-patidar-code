# Step 1: Creating a GitHub Account

> **⏱️ Time Required:** 5–10 minutes | **Difficulty:** Very Easy | **What You Need:** An email address

---

## What Is GitHub?

**GitHub** is a website where developers and students store, share, and collaborate on code and projects. Think of it as **Google Drive, but specifically for code**.

Your instructor has uploaded all the course materials (notes, lab exercises, examples) on GitHub. You need a GitHub account to:
- ✅ Access and download course materials
- ✅ Submit your lab work and projects
- ✅ Keep a portfolio of your work
- ✅ Collaborate with classmates

---

## Step-by-Step: Create Your GitHub Account

### Step 1: Open the Sign-Up Page

1. Open **Google Chrome** (or any browser)
2. Go to: **https://github.com/signup**

### Step 2: Enter Your Email

1. Type your **email address** (use your personal Gmail or university email)
   - Example: `rahul.sharma@gmail.com`
2. Click **"Continue"**

### Step 3: Create a Password

1. Type a **strong password** (at least 8 characters, mix of letters and numbers)
   - Example: `MyStrongP@ss2026`
2. Click **"Continue"**

### Step 4: Choose a Username

This is your **public identity** on GitHub. Choose wisely — potential employers may see it!

**Good username examples:**
- `rahul-sharma-mu` (your name + university)
- `rahulsharma2026` (your name + batch year)
- `rs-bca-mandsaur` (initials + course + city)

**Avoid:**
- ❌ `xx_dark_coder_xx` (unprofessional)
- ❌ `student123` (too generic)
- ❌ Your roll number alone (not memorable)

### Step 5: Verify Your Account

1. GitHub will ask you to solve a **puzzle** (to prove you're not a robot)
2. Complete the puzzle
3. Click **"Create account"**

### Step 6: Verify Your Email

1. Open your email inbox
2. Find the email from GitHub: **"Please verify your email address"**
3. Click the **verification link** in the email
4. You're done! 🎉

---

## Setting Up Your Profile (Optional but Recommended)

A good profile makes you look professional:

1. Click your **profile photo** (top-right corner) → **"Your profile"**
2. Click **"Edit profile"**
3. Fill in:
   - **Name:** Your full name (e.g., "Rahul Sharma")
   - **Bio:** `BCA Semester II Student at Mandsaur University`
   - **Location:** `Mandsaur, Madhya Pradesh, India`
4. Click **"Save"**

## What Not to Do

- ❌ Do not create multiple accounts — one is enough
- ❌ Do not share your password with anyone
- ❌ Do not use your university's computer lab password as your GitHub password
